## Stelle
Here is the [Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3HpMUdepw9s), audio quality is not the best.. I used a Tenor Usb-mic.
The main chord is the `Aadd9` obtained trasposing the `E` with capo at 5th, then the 3th at the bass and finally `Dmaj7`.
